Who We Are

Punyamitra Foundation is a registered NGO committed to empowering underserved communities through holistic development.

Our History

Punyamitra Foundation was founded in 2022 with the goal of creating equal opportunities for underserved communities. What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a movement touching thousands of lives across 8 states in India.

Vision

A world where every individual lives with dignity, access to basic rights, and hope.

Values

Transparency · Empathy · Commitment to Impact
